BHAGAVATH GEETHA  

Relieves one from bondage

 INTRODUCTION 

  • Bhagavath Geetha was told by Lord Krishna who is none other than but the incarnation of Bhagavan Narayana himself to Arjuna, one of the sons of Kunthi. Vyaasa who is an ancient Indian saint wrote Bhagavath Geetha as told by Krishna in the middle of MahaBharatha, an epic happened in India around 5200 years ago. Bhagavath Geetha removes the duality of mind and makes one conflict free and is comprised of 18 chapthers. When integrated with life, it relieves one from bondage like a mother as it gives step by step and very simple solution.

  • One who is reading Bhagavath Geetha should be eternally grateful to Vyaasa Maharshi for his broadened vision to ensure that Bhagavath Geetha should be available to everyone through this writings of MahaBharatha which is like the oil of the lamp whre as Bhagavath Geetha is like the radiance of the lamp.

  • For the one who surrenders, Bhagavath Geetha is like a KalpaVruksha(The magical tree which fulfills all the desires). For that one, Lord Krishna become a cowman whith a stick in hand and milks the nectar of Geetha and poses Gnana mudra.
